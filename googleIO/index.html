Google I/O Connect

Event branding and visual design for Google's developer conference.

Type
Graphic Design
Role
Graphic Designer
Timeline
05/2024 - 08/2024
Team
Brian Long, Kit Meng, DM Zheng, Moira Huang

For Google I/O Connect 2024, our goal was to create an engaging event for developers while showcasing the capabilities of Google Gemini AI.

We created out-of-home (OOH) visuals ahead of the event to build excitement and awareness.

We developed a playful interactive game, "智趣蹴鞠" (AI Penalty Kick), powered by Google Gemini AI to engage attendees in a fun and creative way.
