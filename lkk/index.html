The advertising team working on Lee Kum Kee campaigns faced inefficiency:
each new project involved rebuilding assets from scratch.
This led to inconsistent visuals and wasted effort.

We made a Figma design system using the atomic method, starting with small elements, combining them into components, and then building full templates, to brought all the styles together and made Lee Kum Kee campaigns faster, easier, and more consistent.
