Nomi

A Chrome extension for ADHD to capture tiny thoughts from any page, lets AI organize them, and turns them into actionable calendar events.

Type
AI Product Design
Role
Product Designer
Timeline
2025
Team
Moira Huang, Muhan Liu, Jonathan Zhai

Instant Note Capture

Click the extension and start typing immediately. No tab switching. No onboarding. No friction.

Smart Categories

Notes are automatically categorized by AI. Users can easily rename, add, or remove categories to match their mental model.

Add to Google Calendar

Turn notes into todos and add them to Google Calendar in a single click.

Context as a Bookmark

Each note saves the URL of the page where it was created, allowing users to revisit the original context later.

History & Editing

View and manage past notes—edit content, update categories, add details, favorite important items, or delete what's no longer needed.
