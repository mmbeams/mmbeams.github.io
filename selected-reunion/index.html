Problem

after graduation, are you having trouble connecting with alumni?

Moved to a new city

how do you find people to hang out or play with?

Have a startup idea

Been out of college for a while

Just graduated

how do you connect with alumni in your target industry for coffee chats?

Solution

Bento UI

Modern, clear layout to organize countdown, speakers, social, and overview.

Dot Interaction

Hover animation adds a tech style and separates each category.

Tech Fonts

Fonts give a tech feel and match the event tone.

Multi-layered KV graphic

KV layers shift subtly with mouse direction for parallax depth.

Dot transition

Dots animate out and in during auto-scroll transitions.

Gradient title fade-in

Titles fade in over Huawei gradient text backgrounds.

Horizontal scroll text

Vertical page scroll drives horizontal text movement.
